About Corpus

Corpus is a collection of written texts, especially the entire works of a particular author or a body of writing on a particular subject. In plural, corpus is known as corpora.In our project, we used corpus from Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka and Malay Concordance Project. We crawled the content of both website become one-stop corpus that can search by the user by using only one website.If the user want to search the words, it will appear the usage of words followed by the meaning. The words is from Malay Concordance Project website and the meaning is from Dewan Bahasa dan Pustaka.
